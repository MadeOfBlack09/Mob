BMW by M.O.B is an online shop with her specialty on BMW SUVs, trucks, sedans, lorries, and cars.

Bayerische Motoren Werke AG, commonly abbreviated to BMW is a German multinational manufacturer of luxury vehicles and motorcycles headquartered in Munich, Bavaria, Germany.
The company was founded in 1916 as a manufacturer of aircraft engines, which it produced from 1917 to 1918 and again from 1933 to 1945.

Different type of BMW models

  1. Sedan
  2. Hatchback
  3. SUV

BMW has different vehicles ragging from the hatchback to the sedan to the SUV, and every of these vehicles function differently.

BMW Sedan:

A BMW sedan will always have seating for five, while a four-seat arrangement is generally what drivers will find when they get behind the wheel of a BMW coupe.

BMW Hatchback:

BMW builds hatchbacks across a variety of its model families, including the 3 Series, 4 Series, and 6 Series. If you do not know which one will suit your needs, test drive them all!

BMW SUV:

Versatile vehicles at home on the open road and unexplored terrain. BMW's SUVs combine space, technology, and style in an exceptional sport utility vehicle.
